Terms of Service

Effective date: June 19, 2026

Service

Fiesta La Party Automatic is the official content publishing assistant for Fiesta La Party, a Latin party music project. The service helps the authorized Fiesta La Party team prepare promotional short-form videos, captions, hashtags, and social media drafts.

TikTok Integration

When TikTok authorization is enabled, the service may use TikTok Login Kit and TikTok Content Posting API to upload content as drafts for the authorized account. The authorized account owner or Fiesta La Party team remains responsible for reviewing, editing, and publishing final posts in TikTok.

Acceptable Use

The service must be used only for lawful promotional content that Fiesta La Party owns or has permission to use, including music, artwork, videos, captions, hashtags, and other creative materials.

The service must not be used to publish misleading, infringing, abusive, illegal, adult, or platform-violating content.

Passwords

The service does not request or store TikTok passwords. Access should be granted through TikTok authorization.

Creator Review

Content uploaded through the TikTok integration is intended to be saved as a draft first. The authorized account owner or Fiesta La Party team should review all text, video, music, hashtags, and settings before publishing.

Third-Party Platforms

TikTok and other social platforms have their own terms, policies, and review requirements. Fiesta La Party Automatic is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by TikTok.

Changes

These terms may be updated as the Fiesta La Party publishing workflow changes.

Contact

Questions about this service can be directed to the Fiesta La Party project owner or administrator through the official connected social account.
