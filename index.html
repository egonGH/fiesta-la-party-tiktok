Fiesta La Party Automatic

A private creator workflow for generating short-form promotional content for Fiesta La Party, a Latin party music project.

What It Does

Fiesta La Party Automatic helps the creator prepare TikTok draft videos and social posts for new songs, releases, and music promotion.

The TikTok integration is designed to use TikTok authorization and the Content Posting API to send content as drafts. The creator reviews and publishes final posts inside TikTok.
