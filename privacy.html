Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 17, 2026

Information Used

Fiesta La Party Automatic may use basic TikTok profile information, such as account identity needed for authorization, and upload permissions granted by the creator.

How Information Is Used

Information is used only to authenticate the creator and prepare or upload TikTok draft content for Fiesta La Party promotion.

Passwords

The service does not request, collect, or store TikTok passwords. TikTok access should be handled through TikTok authorization.

Sharing

Information is not sold. Data is shared with TikTok only as needed to use TikTok authorization and content posting features requested by the creator.

Retention

Authorization data, drafts, and generated content should be kept only as long as needed to operate the creator workflow.

Contact

Privacy questions can be directed to the Fiesta La Party creator or administrator.
