Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 19, 2026

Overview

Fiesta La Party Automatic is the official content publishing assistant for Fiesta La Party, a Latin party music project. This policy explains how information is handled when an authorized Fiesta La Party account connects to TikTok and prepares social media drafts.

Information Used

The service may use basic TikTok profile information supplied through TikTok Login Kit, such as the authorized account identity, display name, avatar, and access permissions granted by the account owner.

The service may also process generated promotional materials, captions, hashtags, draft videos, upload status, and technical logs needed to operate the publishing workflow.

How Information Is Used

Information is used to authenticate the authorized account, prepare Fiesta La Party promotional content, upload videos as TikTok drafts, and help the authorized team review content before publishing.

Passwords

The service does not request, collect, or store TikTok passwords. TikTok access is handled through TikTok authorization screens and access tokens.

Sharing

Information is not sold. Data is shared with TikTok only as needed to use TikTok authorization and content posting features requested by the authorized account.

Retention

Authorization data, drafts, generated content, and logs are kept only as long as reasonably needed to operate, troubleshoot, and improve the Fiesta La Party publishing workflow, unless a longer period is required by law or platform policy.

User Choices

The authorized TikTok account owner can revoke TikTok access through TikTok account settings or by contacting the Fiesta La Party project owner or administrator.

Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect authorization data and generated content from unauthorized access.

Contact

Privacy questions can be directed to the Fiesta La Party project owner or administrator through the official connected social account.
